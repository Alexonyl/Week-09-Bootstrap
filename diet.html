Mini Pig Diet



Mini Pigs should be fed a varied and balanced diet, a minimum of 2-3 times daily. Pigs are omnivores that consume fruits, vegetables and animal protein. Typically, wild pigs like to graze and eat small meals about 7-11 times a day. There are pig chow formulas available that are formulated to meet pig nutritional needs but fresh fruit and vegetables should also be included for added nutrition.
