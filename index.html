Mini Pigs

Mini pigs are a breed a pig that reach between 70-200 pounds. Adult mini pigs can reach a height of 14-20 inches. These pigs are a lot smaller than some breeds that can reach up to 600 pounds, but aren't a small dog size as some tend to think.

These pigs typically live around 14-21 years. To reach the maximum lifespan, a balanced diet must be given as well as proper exercise and medical care.

Pigs are very smart animals and are actually found to be more intelligent than dogs. This does not mean they are easy to train, however. The tend to be stubborn and can be aggressive to try to assert dominance. These pigs are typically prey animals, so it takes time and effort to gain their trust and earn their affection.
